Tips for New Baseball Coaches:

  • Have a plan for practices.
  • Go over all the fundamentals.
  • Don't be afraid to try things from drills or approach.
  • Have plenty of baseballs, gloves, helmets, bats on hand for practices and games.
  • Talk to the kids using easy to understand words. They are just now learning how to play.
  • Ask for help from other Coaches, Parents and Volunteers.
  • Give kuddos to players as much as possible.
  • Be Enthusiastic, Patient, and Fair.
  • Most Important: Have Fun.
